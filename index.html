About Overlake

The Overlake School is a community-focused independent school located in Redmond, Washington. Overlake emphasizes rigorous academics, experiential learning, and development of ethical leadership. Students find a balance of challenging coursework, arts and athletics, and opportunities for service and research.

  • • Focus: College-prep, interdisciplinary learning

Academics

Overlake offers a robust college-preparatory curriculum with opportunities for advanced coursework, project-based learning, and independent study. Small class sizes foster close student-teacher interaction and collaborative inquiry.

STEM & Math

Computer science, lab sciences, and mathematics pathways geared toward problem solving and research.

Humanities

Literature, history, and language are thoroughly studied in vairous classes.

Global Learning

Experiential opportunities, cultural exchange, and community-engaged projects.

Arts

A vibrant arts program supports visual arts, music, theater, and design. Students showcase work in galleries and productions throughout the year.

Athletics

Overlake fields competitive teams and encourages wide participation in athletics and wellness programs. Sports build teamwork, resilience, and school spirit.
