About Overlake

Founded in 1967, The Overlake School is an independent, nonsectarian, coeducational day school located on a 73-acre campus in Redmond, WA. Overlake’s mission is to champion students and their vast potential through engaging educational experiences in a diverse and inclusive community.

Academics

Overlake offers a rigorous college-preparatory program with small class sizes, 17 AP and honors courses, and a strong emphasis on experiential education, critical thinking, and student-centered learning.

Arts at Overlake

Students explore visual arts, theater, stagecraft, music, digital design, and more. The arts program encourages creativity, collaboration, and community expression.

Athletics

Overlake supports a robust athletics program including soccer, basketball, cross-country, tennis, volleyball, track and field, and more. Athletics foster teamwork, resilience, and school spirit.

Admissions

Overlake welcomes applications from families seeking a supportive, challenging, and inclusive learning environment. Tuition assistance and financial aid are available.
